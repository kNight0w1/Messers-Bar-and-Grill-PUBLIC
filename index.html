Escape the Mundane
at
Messers

Messers isn't your run-of-the-mill eatery. Located directly off U.S Highway 231 upon entering Linden, Indiana Northbound, perhaps the first thing to catch your eye will be the Messer's wall painting on the front of an otherwise inconspicous two story shack. But messers is special. Housing original artwork done by local artists, great food, live events, and open mics, amenities such as pool, darts, etc.., and above all an amazing community of people. "I hope this can be a special place for friends and strangers alike to meet, eat, drink, and above all just have a good time." (Jeff Messer)
